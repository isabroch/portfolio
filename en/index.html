About me

Why choose me?

I have completed the web development (webudvikler) course at Roskilde Tekniske Skole. I am deeply fascinated by technology and what can be accomplished with it.

I have also completed the basic course for media design (mediegrafiker) at NEXT København.

I’ve studied and been interested in both design and web development since I was 10 years old. Learning is my passion, and I’m always eager to pick up new skills and improve myself.

Who am I?

My name is Isabella Broch. I’m a woman born in 1999 and am half-Danish, half-Filipino. I lived in the Philippines and Cambodia for most of my childhood, studying in international schools and learning English. In 2013, I came back to Denmark for high school and have lived here ever since!

I love puzzles and the struggle of figuring them out. That’s why I love coding, and especially web development. The feeling when you have a problem and find a solution that actually works… I love it! It’s visual feedback that logic works.

In my free time, I like to read articles about the latest web developments, read fantasy novels, play video games, and play with my two adorable cats Inu og Zorro.

My future?

I am always looking to improve my skills within JAMStack, but I’m especially interested in learning other tools to improve my workflow such as React, Vue, and Typescript. I am also interested in learning about Python, as it has become so popular with many programmers.
